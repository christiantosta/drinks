# RECIPE: CERRADO SUNRISE (REFRESHING GIN OR VODKA SOUR)

## SERVING RECOMMENDATIONS:

* Glass Type: Long Glass (Collins or Highball).
* Ice: Cubed ice, used in preparation and serving.

## INGREDIENTS:

* Gin or Vodka: 1 1/2 shots
* Orange Juice: 1/2 cup (approx. 3.5 - 4 oz)
* Sparkling Water: 1/2 cup (approx. 3.5 - 4 oz)
* Pomegranate Syrup: 1/2 to 1 tablespoon (Grenadine or Redcurrant, to taste)
* Ice: To taste

## PREPARATION METHOD:

1.  Combine: In a cocktail shaker, add the ice, gin (or vodka), orange juice,
    and pomegranate syrup.
2.  Shake: Shake vigorously in the shaker until the mixture is well chilled and
    slightly aerated.
3.  Serve: Pour the contents (strained) into a long glass filled with ice.
4.  Top Up: Complete the glass with sparkling water.

## GARNISH:

* Use an orange wheel or half-moon slice on the rim of the glass.
