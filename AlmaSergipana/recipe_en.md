# RECIPE: ALMA SERGIPANA (ENHANCED CAIPIRINHA)

## SERVING RECOMMENDATIONS:

* Glass Type: Highball Glass or Large Goblet.
* Ice: Cubed ice, used in preparation and serving.

## INGREDIENTS:

* Artisanal Cachaça: 1 1/2 shots
* Limes: 2 units (sliced)
* Sugar: 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
* Cashew Juice: 1/2 American cup (approx. 100-120 ml)
* Coconut Milk: 1 shot
* Ice: To taste

## PREPARATION METHOD:

1.  Muddle: In a cocktail shaker, add the lime slices and sugar. Muddle gently
    to extract the juice and oils from the zest.
2.  Add Liquids: Add the ice, artisanal cachaça, and coconut milk.
3.  Shake and Serve: Shake vigorously in the shaker until the mixture is well
    chilled. Pour the contents (strained or unstrained) into the Highball glass.
4.  Top Up: Slowly add the cashew juice, stirring, until the glass level is
    complete.

## GARNISH:

* Use a mint sprig, a lime wheel, or a small cut of cashew pulp.
